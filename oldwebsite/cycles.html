The cycle of low self esteem

As portrayed above the cycle of low self esteem is a self fulfilling prophecy in it of itself. It creates the conditions that enact out own torture and creates low self esteem.

Self fulfilling prophecies

Self fulfilling prophecies are when your beliefs affect your actions toward the world and eventually create the conditions that you believed would take place.

Core self-esteem vs. Situational self-esteem

Core self esteem is the self esteem that you hold withinm, Situational self-esteem is self worth gained from actions and mastery in areas of your life.

Early Life experiences

Early life experiences shape who we are in alot of ways. Of course we still have control over where our lives really go but understanding our early life experiences gives us more leverage in the fight against our insticts.
