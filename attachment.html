Past experience and your attachment style

Your early childhood experiences affect your attachment style so having a good understand of your childhood helps to understand the style that you have

How your attachment style affects your future relationships

Understanding your attachment style and the positives and negatives that come with it is important because it can lead us to fall into patterns of failure in our relationships if we are not careful.

Healing with self disclosure

Self disclosure is the amount of yourself that you show to others. Engaging with close ones with high self disclosure can be a positive and healthy way to get help.

Concious effort can change them

Your attachment style can be changed over time with counscious effort but that can only happen when you know your attachment style.

Tools

  • Activities to express emotion
  • Being more expressive with you communication
  • Being aware of your attachment style
  • Practice being more open with your emotions with those you feel most comfortable
  • Put effort into change
