Addiction

Addiction comes in many forms. Pay attention to what your doing everyday because those are the most important things that you do in your life.

Technology and distraction

Technology and social media is often a coping mechanism that we pay little attention to but when we lose it we see much more clearly that we are using it to escape from reality.

Stress

Stress is often seen as a negative thing and in a lot of ways it is a negative thing but if we frame it in a positive way we can use it to improve our lives.

Burnout

Burnout comes after a long period of extended stress. It really takes everything out of you and is very damaging in the long term. Pay attention to when you feel consistently worn out and take time to recover and relax.
